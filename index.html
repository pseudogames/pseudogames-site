About

Hi, zED here. Pseudogames is a project, a fraternity of friends and colleagues that assemble from time to time on game jams.

For the good or for the bad, the people involved have no other intention than to make the game and see other people playing it.

Events

2011 - 2014

SPJAM
meet other teams

Was one of the best jams to be in Sao Paulo, there were no competition besides the queues that formed to play the games on the arcade at the exposition. Most people have teams when they arrive, but it's possible to join one there.

Lately the overflow of people (about 400 people each edition) forced the event in a downward spiral, for instance there is no arcade or expo anymore. But, even then, the opportunity of an immediate feedback from the other game makers is quite interesting.

2013

Ludum Dare
one man army

At this one you must do the game all alone. It's the hardest one to participate, but it could be a nice opportinity to try something new, without much conflict or comminication problems.

The game is only displayed online, but there is quite some people trying and commenting on it.

2013

Global Game Jam
meet other people

The objective is to go alone and form a team there. It's the best jam I went to test your communication skills, thats the hardest part on this one.
