hardheaded Who will be the last man standing?!
  • Submitted

A one flag CTF style game developed during SPJAM 2013 game marathon, São Paulo, Brazil. The main theme were "plot twist" and the random onws were "head" and "yellow". We choose to make an heavily copyright sensitive hommage to indiana jones, allan quarterman, with graphics from zelda. As it's not an commercial game, we hope it fits under fair use, any complaint get in contact with us through pseudogames-dev at google groups. It was implemented using C as main language and SDL as graphic library, during the 48h marathon, with some late bugfixes on the following week. Originally it was designed to be played as an arcade game with multiplayer support. The plot is that both characters are after the golden head, and the twist it the change from a dispute to a cooperation to kill the threatening wave of zombies that come once in a while. Game makers: Carlo "zED" Caputo, Pedro Mariano; special thanks to Maxwell Dayvson for the sfx implementation.

A survivor style game developed during SPJAM 2012 game marathon, São Paulo, Brazil. The first version of the game (check the Alpha video) was fully developed in just 48 hours. inferno because zombies must die.
  • Gameplay
  • Arcade
  • Marathon

At SPJAM 2011 we did our first work as pseudogames. An infinite map, countless living-deads, a flamethower (that have weirdly alive flames, btw) and an urge to stay alive in the middle of this rocky place full of lava pools. It was on a public arcade machine for a week after the event. SDL/C Code: Carlo "zED" Caputo, Fernando Meyer; Graphic design: Cristine Ronchi.
