Inferno

At SPJAM 2011 we did our first work as pseudogames. An infinite map, countless living-deads, a flamethower (that have weirdly alive flames, btw) and an urge to stay alive in the middle of this rocky place full of lava pools. It was on a public arcade machine for a week after the event.

  • Jam: SPJAM 2011
  • Themes: mist, fire, infinite.
  • Game makers:
    • Carlo "zED" Caputo: programmer, game design
    • Fernando Meyer: programmer, music
    • Cristine Ronchi: art
  • Platform: C, SDL
  • Links:
    • Source on Github
    • Download Windows Game (9M)
  • Controls:
    • Up, Down, Left, Right: Move
    • Space: Fire
    • Escape: Start / Menu
